SME Invoice System

{# Lead paragraph: high-level description for non-technical readers #}

A lightweight web application that helps small and medium-sized enterprises manage invoices, follow up payments and gain a clearer view of their cash flow.

{# Additional context explaining the scope and design decisions: - focus on core functions, - configurable rules (tax, currency), - position as a support tool for digitalisation. #}

The prototype focuses on core functions: customer management, invoice creation, simple analytics and configurable business rules such as tax rate and currency. It is designed as a transparent, adaptable support tool for SME digitalisation, rather than a full commercial invoicing product.
